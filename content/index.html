What Is Formula 1?

Formula 1, is the highest level of international auto racing for single-seater formula racing cars. Drivers compete for the F1 Drivers' Championship, while teams compete for the F1 Constructors' Championship. F1 cars are the world's fastest regulated road-course racing cars. They use electronics, aerodynamics, suspension, and tires to achieve high cornering speeds.
